Our Process

Our Process

from beginning to end

Our process first starts with coagulation and flocculation. In Coagulation, there are positively charged chemicals that have other chemicals added to them to neutralize any chemicals that may be negative. Flocculation then occurs. This mixture bonds particles together. Then the next stage sedimentation takes place as everything settles at the bottom of the water. This happens is large basins and there are multiple sedimentation processes to filter our water as much as possible. Once this process is done the filtration process takes place. This process is to filter the small particles that may not have had any chemical reaction and are still floating around in the water. The filtration process we use also happens multiple times to ensure the water is 100% clean to drink. The last stage is disinfection. In our water, we use chlorine to disinfect the water. This will not harm anyone and has been used since the 1800s.
